## Project Review – Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) Website

This project presents the design and development of a simple brand identity and responsive homepage for the **Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)**, an industry-led institute focused on digital, cyber and AI risk training.

The objective of the project was to create a clean, modern and professional digital presence that communicates IDR’s mission of developing skilled digital risk practitioners. The design uses a minimalist technology-inspired aesthetic with a primary colour palette of **orange, white and black** to represent innovation, trust and resilience.

The logo is based on a **cube-inspired geometric shape**, symbolising structure, stability and risk management in the digital ecosystem. Two variants of the logo were created: an icon-only version suitable for small interfaces such as favicons, and a full logo including the institute name for branding and web use.

The website was developed using **semantic HTML5, CSS and vanilla JavaScript**, ensuring accessibility, responsive design and smooth navigation across desktop and mobile devices. Key sections such as the hero area, institutional overview, service pillars, community focus and contact call-to-action clearly communicate the role of IDR in training, innovation and advisory services.

Overall, the project demonstrates the integration of **basic branding, user interface design and front-end web development** to create a functional and visually consistent landing page for a technology-focused training institute.
